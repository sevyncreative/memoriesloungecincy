WHAT IS THE DRESS CODE

What is the dress code? Upscale fashion attire. Swimwear, sports/athletic apparel, and casual loungewear is not permitted. Dress code guidelines are maintained at the venues discretion.

CAN I BRING LARGE BAGS?

The use of oversized bags, backpacks, and purses are not permitted. Small purses are allowed, all bags subject to search prior to entry.

IS THERE A COAT CHECK OR BAG STORAGE?

Yes, coat check is available inside our venue by the restrooms.

HOW CAN I RETRIEVE LOST ITEMS?

During hours of operation lost items will be at the admissions desk located at front entrance. After hours, lost items will be turned over to hotel lost & found.
